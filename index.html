Challenges in Urban Food Waste Management

Excessive Food Waste

Urban households generate tons of food waste daily, most of which ends up in landfills, leading to environmental hazards.

Limited Recycling Facilities

Urban areas often lack efficient recycling or composting systems, making food waste disposal a significant challenge.

Health and Environmental Hazards

Improper waste management releases greenhouse gases, contributing to global warming and impacting public health.
