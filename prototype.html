Prototype of the Waste-to-Energy System

Prototyping allows designers to create physical or digital models of the solution to test ideas, explore functionality, and visualize how the final product will work. The prototyping phase played a critical role in the development of our composting system.

Anaerobic Digestion Tank

This prototype was tested to evaluate how effectively it processes food waste in a confined space while maintaining the necessary temperature, moisture, and airflow.

Biogas Collection System

The team built a small-scale version of the biogas generator to measure energy output and assess its ability to power apartment complex areas.

Composting Output

Testing the quality of the digestate allowed us to refine the composting process to ensure it could be used in urban gardening and other applications.

Impact: The prototype phase provided key insights into the system's capacity, helped optimize energy production, and ensured the solution was user-friendly. The feedback from prototype testing allowed the team to refine the final design, making the solution more efficient and effective.
